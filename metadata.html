Identifier prisoners_overview.zip
Title Jailodbreak - Prisoners Overview
Description This dataset contains data from three organizations: Eurostat, UNECE (United Nations Economic Commission for Europe) and UNODC (United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime). The dataset collects information relating to the characteristics of the prison population in Europe. Specifically, it provides information on: gender, age (adult / juvenile), legal status, citizenship (citizen / foreigner). All source datasets are divided by country covering almost all of the European states. The temporal coverage differs. As regards the dataset from UNECE, "Prisoners by Citizenship, Sex, Measurement, Country and Year", it covers the years 1980 to 2019; "Prisoners by Age and Sex - number and rate for the relevant sex and age groups", by Eurostat instead covers the years from 2008 to 2019. Concerning the "Persons held by status" dataset from UNODC (United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime), the time coverage is from 2010 to 2019.
